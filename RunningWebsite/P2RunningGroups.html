Running Groups

Welcome to our comprehensive guide to running groups in North-Holland! Whether you're a seasoned marathoner or just lacing up your shoes for the first time, this page is designed to connect you with local running communities that suit your goals and fitness level. From relaxed social jogs along scenic routes to more structured sessions for those with a competitive edge, we’ve got something for everyone. Discover the perfect running group that matches your pace, meet like-minded runners, and join in on the fun while staying motivated and reaching your goals!

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower runners of all abilities by creating a supportive and engaging community. Through organized running groups, exciting competitions, and expert tips, we aim to promote a healthy and active lifestyle. We believe in the power of running to build connections, improve well-being, and help each runner achieve their personal goals—whether you're a beginner finding your stride or a seasoned athlete chasing new milestones. Together, we strive to push our limits, celebrate progress, and foster a love for running that lasts a lifetime.
